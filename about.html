Why Choose Us?

Rishi Aurobindo Memorial School has a record of excellence from the year 1872.
Established with the vision to educate the new generation, the school is continuing every year the perpetual quest in the service of the nation.

Over the centuries, our school has constantly upgraded course curriculum and campus. Moreover, adapting to the modern fast changing world has become our motto; thus in the present digital age we aim to best utilize and teach technology.

  • CISCE Affiliation (number: WB296)
  • Modern Computer and Science Lab
  • Streams: PCM, Arts, PCB & Commerce
  • Sports and Hostel Facilities
  • Worhshops for Communication skills development, etc
  • Annual tours across city's historically significant centres
